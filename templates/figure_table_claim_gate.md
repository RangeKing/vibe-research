# Figure/Table Claim Gate

```text
figure_or_table_id:
panel_or_section_id:
one_sentence_message:
claim_supported:
metric:
unit_or_class:
comparison_basis:
effect_size:
denominator:
uncertainty:
uncertainty_excluded:
sample/model/case basis:
scope:
source_data_or_method_pointer:
visual_or_structural_failure:
required_revision:
```

Five-second central-result test:

- Can a reader identify the central result within five seconds?
- Are effect size, denominator, comparison basis, uncertainty, scope, and source/method traceability visible?
- Does the figure/table carry the central claim rather than only provenance, workflow, inputs, dashboard inventory, or decorative context?
