## Rules

- Figure 1 should establish the conceptual or empirical setup only if it creates a result, not a decorative map.
- The central answer figure must appear by Figure 2 or Figure 3 unless the paper has a strong experimental sequence.
- Provenance, input fields, data coverage and sensitivity dashboards usually belong in Extended Data or Supplementary Information unless they alter the main conclusion.
- A six-figure package must not contain more than one provenance figure in the main text.
